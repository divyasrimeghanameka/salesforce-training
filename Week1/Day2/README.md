# Day 2 - Salesforce Platform Fundamentals

## What is the Salesforce Platform?
The Salesforce Platform is a cloud-based system that helps businesses create, customize, and manage applications such as CRM solutions and other business tools.

---

## Important Concepts

### App
An App is a set of related tabs, objects, and functionalities designed for a particular business process.

**Examples:**
- Sales App
- Service App

---

### Object
An Object works like a database table that stores specific types of records and information.

**Examples:**
- Account
- Contact
- Opportunity

---

### Tab
A Tab allows users to open and access objects or features directly from the Salesforce interface.

**Example:**
- Account Tab → Opens Account records

---

## Relationship Between CRM and Salesforce Platform

CRM data such as Accounts, Contacts, and Opportunities are stored as Salesforce Objects within different Apps.

### Examples
- **Account** → Stores company details
- **Contact** → Stores customer or person details
- **Opportunity** → Represents a potential sales deal

---

## Configuration vs Development

### Configuration (No-Code Approach)
Used when business requirements can be achieved without programming.

**Examples:**
- Creating custom fields
- Modifying page layouts
- Setting validation rules

---

### Development using Apex (Coding)
Used when advanced or complex business logic is required.

**Examples:**
- Custom automation
- External API integrations
- Advanced business processes
